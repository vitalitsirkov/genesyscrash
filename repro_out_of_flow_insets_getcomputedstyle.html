Repro: out-of-flow getComputedStyle insets crash

Targets blink::ComputedStyleUtils::ValueForPositionOffset in computed_style_utils.cc. Without the !box->GetLayoutResults().empty() guard, calling OutOfFlowInsetsForGetComputedStyle() on a fragmentless out-of-flow box hits a CHECK (or worse, a UAF on layout_results_.front()) and crashes the renderer.

To reach the buggy path the inset query must NOT take the early "fixed length & no position-fallback" shortcut. So we use %, auto, calc(), or fixed + position-try-fallbacks.

To leave the OOF box with empty GetLayoutResults() we wrap it in content-visibility: hidden (a "display lock"), which keeps the LayoutBox alive but skips its layout — even after a forced layout flush via document.body.offsetTop.
