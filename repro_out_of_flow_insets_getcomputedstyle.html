Repro: OOF getComputedStyle insets crash

Targets blink::ComputedStyleUtils::ValueForPositionOffset. Without the !box->GetLayoutResults().empty() guard, OutOfFlowInsetsForGetComputedStyle() is called on a box that has never been laid out, hitting CHECK(!layout_results.empty()).

The two conditions needed to crash:

  1. The OOF element's inset is not a fixed length (so the early if (offset.IsFixed() && !MayHavePositionFallbackList()) short-circuit does NOT fire). Use top: auto, %, or calc().
  2. The OOF box has never been laid out, so GetLayoutResults() is empty. This happens in the multicol / fragmented-container scenario (bug 1473508) and in the inline-script-before- first-layout scenario below.
